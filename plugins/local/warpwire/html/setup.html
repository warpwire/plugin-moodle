Warpwire needs to be configured.

Please ask your Moodle administrator to perform the following steps:

  • Go to Site administration > Plugins > Warpwire Plugin Configuration
  • Option 1: Press the "Start Trial" button to automatically configure Warpwire
  • Option 2: Manually enter in the Warpwire credentials information
  • Save your changes

Once the Warpwire configuratation is complete, this page will load your Warpwire content.
